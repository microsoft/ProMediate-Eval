## Issue 1: Water Prediction and Audit of Water Withdrawal and Use
Key concerns:  
- Who should be responsible for annual water predictions? 
- Who should be responsible for auditing annual water withdrawal and use?  
- What sort of fines should be imposed for overwithdrawals, and should audits look at past data? 

## Issue 2: Unused Water Allocations
Key concerns:  
- Who should decide what happens to any unused water allocations? 
- Who should benefit from unused water allocations?

## Issue 3: Environmental Flows

Key concerns:  
- Should water be allocated to the environment, even during times of low flows? 
- If so, who should bear the responsibility of providing this water?
