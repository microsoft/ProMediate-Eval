## Issue 1: Water Prediction and Audit of Water Withdrawal and Use

- Option 1A: An independent predictions and auditing department.
- Option 1B: An independent prediction body paired with a new audit department overseen by the Ministerial Council.
- Option 1C: A new multistate prediction and auditing body.
- Option 1D: An independent body predicts water flow, and the Basin Authority conducts auditing.

## Issue 2: Unused Water Allocations

- Option 2A: Give unused allocations to the environment.
- Option 2B: Excess water should flow to downstream states.
- Option 2C: Northland should have the option of auctioning off its excess water or storing it for future use.
- Option 2D: Basin Authority should redistribute water.

## Issue 3: Environmental Flows

- Option 3A: All states should contribute equally.
- Option 3B: The lowest riparian should provide environmental flows.
- Option 3C: Ignore the environment for now.
